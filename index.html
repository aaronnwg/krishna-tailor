Krishna Tailor Khao Lak

Welcome to Krishna Tailor Khao Lak, where tradition, craftsmanship, and luxury converge. Nestled in the heart of Khao Lak, Thailand, our family-owned and operated shop has proudly served clients for over a decade, delivering exquisite, custom-made garments tailored to perfection. Led by Krishna, a master tailor with years of experience, we are dedicated to meeting the unique sartorial desires of each customer with precision and care. At Krishna Tailor, we believe in the art of personalized fashion, ensuring every stitch reflects elegance, sophistication, and timeless style. Experience the pinnacle of bespoke tailoring with us.

Contact Krishna in Khao Lak to book your appointment today.

Services Offered

At Krishna Tailor Khao Lak, we specialize in crafting an array of custom-made garments, tailored to your exact specifications. Whether you're looking for a perfectly fitted suit, an elegant tuxedo, a refined blazer, or a bespoke shirt or pair of pants, our expertise extends to all aspects of men's and women's clothing.
